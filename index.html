Project Manager, Civil and Structural Engineer

Keshab is a Project Manager with more than 10 years of experience across the residential, civil, building and infrastructure sectors on the diverse range of projects. He has extensive experience across all facets of operations, from project inception & development, design coordination and planning, organizing, and managing construction activities, to client, stakeholder and community engagement. Keshab has strong communication skills with excellent attention to detail and is dedicated to delivering projects of the highest calibre and forging longstanding relationships with clients. He possesses a proven track record of preparing and executing project plans and programmes, ensuring that work is carried out in accordance with the Australian Standards, companies procedures and clients satisfaction and has been able to ensure timely, safe and cost effective design and implementation during the life cycle of projects.
